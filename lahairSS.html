 Our Services

Fine European & Bleached Texture
At LA Hair, we have a 5-way guarantee: no damage to your hair, no waiting appointments, expert color & texture matching, no tangles or shedding for 6 months and no lumps or bumps!

With our Signature "Micro Tracking System" the hair lays as flat as if it were glued, but of course at LA Hair we never use glue or anything else that will damage your hair. Our method leaves no tell-tell lumps or bumps. Your hair will also have a natural projection

Tracks lasts 3-4 months in any texture of hair with proper care and scheduled maintenance. Our Expert Color & Texture Matching guarantees your texture and color will always match in all types of hair including bi-racial textures and ash blonde colors.

We never pull your hair out with tracks that are too tight and tightening your tracks is quick and easy. The perfect method for busy, glamorous Career, Professional and Business Ladies of all nationalities!
